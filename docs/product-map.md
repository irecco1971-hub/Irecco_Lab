# Irecco Lab — Product Map

## Brand Structure
Irecco Lab is the umbrella brand.
Products under Irecco Lab can target very different markets and may have distinct language, onboarding, and positioning.

## Brand Architecture Model
Recommended model: hybrid / endorsed products.
- Irecco Lab = studio, umbrella, trust layer
- Each product = independent product identity with its own use case and target audience
- Public presentation can use “by Irecco Lab” when helpful

## Product Types
The platform must support products that may differ by:
- industry
- terminology
- monetization
- onboarding flow
- support style
- feature depth

## Current Product Portfolio

### MillWork
Status: anchor product
Role: first product expected to validate the platform model and contribute early revenue
Target: CNC woodworking / SketchUp / production workflow users

## Future Product Rule
Every new product must answer these questions before implementation:
1. Who is the user?
2. What painful workflow does it solve?
3. What is reused from core?
4. What remains product-specific?
5. What is the smallest sellable version?
